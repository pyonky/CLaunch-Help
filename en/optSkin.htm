Skins (Options dialog)
Select a skin to change the appearance of CLaunch.
When you select a skin from the list, it is applied temporarily so you can preview the changes.
Information about the selected skin is shown in the skin information area at the bottom.
Apply the Recommended Settings
Loads the skin's recommended design settings, such as button/tab sizes and the positions of icons and text.
Reset the Text Settings
Restores text-related settings changed after applying a skin to the skin's default settings.
Also resets the Design Pattern selected for each page.
Open the Skins Folder
Opens the folder where the skin files are stored.
