Contact
Bug reports are accepted by e-mail only.
pyonky_claunch@yahoo.co.jp

A response to reported issues cannot be guaranteed.
Issues that cannot be reproduced in the author's environment or that lack clear reproduction steps may not be addressed.
To prevent your message from being filtered as spam, please include the word CLaunch in the subject line.
No reply will be sent unless additional information is required.
When reporting a bug, please include the following information.
  • CLaunch version
  • Description of the issue and steps to reproduce (as detailed as possible)
  • Your environment (Windows version, resident programs other than CLaunch, etc.)
  • Option settings changed from the default state (attaching CLaunch.ini is recommended)
