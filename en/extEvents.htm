Event-Based Launch
You can configure items to be launched automatically when specific events occur.
The following four events are available for automatic launching:
  • When CLaunch starts
  • When CLaunch exits
  • When entering Sleep or Hibernate
  • When resuming from Sleep or Hibernate
Event-based launch settings can be configured in the Events tab of the Options dialog.
Automatic launching at CLaunch startup can be canceled by holding down the Shift key while starting CLaunch.
