Moving Items
Press and hold the button with the item you want to move, then drag it to the desired position.
While dragging, a guide line indicates the insertion position. Release the mouse button when the item is in the desired location.
You can cancel the move operation by pressing the Esc key while dragging.
Copying Items
If you hold down the Ctrl key while dragging an item and keep it pressed until you release the mouse button, the item is copied.
If you copy a submenu, the items inside the submenu are not copied and an empty submenu is created.
If an item with Chain Launch Items configured is copied, the chain launch settings are not inherited by the copied item.
Moving Items Between Pages
While dragging an item, move the cursor over a page tab to switch pages.
After the page switches, drag the item to the desired position and release the mouse button.
Moving Items into Submenus
When you move the cursor over a submenu button while dragging an item, the submenu opens automatically.
Move the cursor within the submenu until an insertion line appears, then release the mouse button at the desired position.
Moving Items Within Submenus
Items in a submenu can be rearranged within the submenu or moved outside the submenu using drag and drop.
You can also move items to a page, or to a parent or child submenu.
While dragging an item, moving the cursor over another submenu button on the page opens that submenu as the move destination.
If multiple menu items are selected, dragging them moves all selected items at once.
Inserting Blank Spaces (Pages Only)
Right-click the button at the position where you want to insert a blank space and select Insert Empty Button from the context menu.
You can also insert a blank space by pressing the Insert key while the cursor is positioned at the desired location.
Deleting Items
Right-click the item you want to delete and select Delete from the context menu.
You can also delete an item by pressing the Delete key while the cursor is positioned over it.
Deleting empty buttons on a page closes the gaps between items.
