Switching and Adding Pages
Items can be grouped by page according to the type of files you register.
Switching Pages
You can switch pages using the following actions.
Clicking a page tab
Rotating the mouse wheel
Shortcut Keys
Mouse-over on a page tab (when enabled in Options)
Regardless of option settings, while dragging files or moving items, pages are automatically switched simply by moving the cursor over a page tab.
Adding Pages
To add a page, select Add Page from the Main Menu or the Page Menu.
When the Add Page dialog appears, enter the name of the new page.
You can add up to 100 pages.
New pages are added to the rightmost position. Move the page as needed.
Reordering Pages
Press and hold the left mouse button on the tab of the page you want to move, then drag it to the desired position.
While dragging, a guide line indicates the insertion position. Release the mouse button at the desired location.
Renaming Pages
Right-click a page tab and select Properties from the displayed menu, or simply double-click the page tab.
In addition to changing the page name, you can also configure per-page design settings here.
