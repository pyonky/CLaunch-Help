Events (Options dialog)
Lets you launch registered items automatically when certain events occur.
Click Configure for each event to open the Auto-start Items dialog.

In the Auto-start Items dialog, click the Add button to show page names in a drop-down list, then select the page or submenu that contains the item you want.
Double-click a cell in the Item column of the added row to show items in the selected page in a drop-down list, then select the item you want.
Double-click a cell in the Delay column of a row that has a launch item to set the wait time before the target item is launched.
You can reorder the list with the Up and Down buttons, but items are not guaranteed to be launched in the displayed order.
On CLaunch startup
Sets items to launch automatically immediately after CLaunch starts.
You can cancel auto-start by launching CLaunch while holding down the Shift key.
On CLaunch exit
Sets items to launch automatically just before CLaunch exits.
On entering sleep or hibernation
Sets items to launch automatically just before the system enters sleep or hibernation.
On resume from sleep or hibernation
Sets items to launch automatically immediately after the system resumes from sleep or hibernation.
