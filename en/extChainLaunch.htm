Launching Multiple Items
If you frequently launch the same combination of multiple items in your daily workflow, you can use Chain Launch Items.
By configuring Chain Launch Items in the Item Properties, multiple items can be launched simultaneously by pressing a single button.

By setting a launch delay for Chain Launch Items, you can control the launch order and the waiting time between each item.
To prevent infinite launch loops, items that are launched via Chain Launch Items cannot trigger another chain launch.
If you want to launch multiple items only temporarily for a one-time task, use Continuous Launch Mode instead.
