Changing the Design
The appearance of each part of the window can be customized using the options listed below.

The width and color of the window frame, the text shown in the title bar, and the window transparency can be changed on the Design 1 tab of the Options dialog.
The size and display contents of tabs and buttons can be changed on the Design 2 tab of the Options dialog.
The background and text colors of tabs and buttons can be changed on the Pages tab of the Options dialog. (Different designs can be set for each page.)
Icons for buttons and menu items can be changed in the Item Properties dialog.
You can also change the appearance by using skins. (Windows 2000 or later)
When Separate designs per View Mode on the Design 2 tab of the Options dialog is enabled, the design automatically switches according to the View Mode.
